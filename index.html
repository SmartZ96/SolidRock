WHAT WE DO


What we do is that we speak to our clients who have suffered from various addictions such as alcohol, drugs, depression and need a little more support with their family.

DROP IN SESSIONS


Our drop ins happen on Tuesday, Thursday and Friday afternoons. Every now and then we have a meeting on Sunday evenings we call Street Church, which involves a meal and someone sharing their story of recovery. We do a Christmas meal every year for our service users and give them a food parcel for over the festive period.
