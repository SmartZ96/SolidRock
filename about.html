MEET PAUL


Paul Innes is the manager of the Solid Rock Cafe and he helps people who has been struggling with addictions such as Alcohol, Drugs, Depression or more support for families.

WHAT IS THE SOLID ROCK CAFE?


The Solid Rock Cafe is an outbreach initiative that provides to those that are desperately in need of help from battling all kinds of addictions, as well as support for families, who often suffer greatly when loved ones become addicted to drugs or something worse.

HOW WE HELP


we offer a host of services which includes drop-in centres and one-on-one appointments, The Solid Rock's dedication team of staff and volunteers work to the best of their ability at building relationships that offers assistance and provides counselling to those who need it desperatley. The Solid Rock Cafe is most often the first port of call for those who are looking to change their lives and to help many people in the UK.

OUR SERVICE USERS


We have anything from 20-50 through our doors on any given week. Our service users are very often addicted to drugs or alcohol, some come along because they are lonely and in need of somewhere to go for some company and support. Our service users build relationships within our projects and have a greater awareness of services available through conversation with our team. Many have gone on to live drug free as a result of coming into contact with us. Others have spoken of a sense of belonging within the café and feel part of something as opposed to being at home feeling lonely.
